Masaai Mara

Masai Mara National Reserve is an area of preserved savannah wilderness in southwestern Kenya, along the Tanzanian borderi famous and popular safari destination in Kenya,it is famous for its leopard, lion, and cheetah, but there's plenty more game to see on the wide-open plains, from giraffe and eland to smaller predators such as bat-eared fox, spotted hyena and black-backed jackal.

Mombasa beautiful Beaches

Mombasa is Kenya's main tourist destination located on the Eastern coastline of Kenya bordering the Indian Ocean which has made it a popular destination famous for its beaches,with world-class beach resorts, and it's historical significance. It is the most popular tourist destination it is the second largest city in Kenya. with beautiful beach resorts and the Old Town, where fragrant spices waft from local markets.
