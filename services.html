KWS services

Respect the privacy of the wildlife, this is their habitat. Beware of the animals, they are wild and can be unpredictable. Don't crowd the animals or make sudden noises or movements. Do not feed the animals, it upsets their diet and leads to human dependence. Please keep quiet, noise disturbs the wildlife. Stay in your vehicle at all times, except at designated picnic or walking areas, consult your driver/tour guide.
